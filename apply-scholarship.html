How to Submit Your Documents

  1. Upload all required documents to Google Drive
  2. Set sharing permissions to "Anyone with the link can view"
  3. Copy the shareable link and paste it below
  4. Ensure all documents are clearly labeled (e.g., "Transcript.pdf", "ID.jpg")
Tip: Organize all documents in a single Google Drive folder for easier sharing
