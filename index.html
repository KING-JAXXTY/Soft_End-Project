About TulongAral+

TulongAral+ is dedicated to bridging the gap between students and scholarship opportunities in Northern Luzon and Metro Manila.

Our mission is to empower Filipino students by providing easy access to verified scholarship programs and connecting them with generous sponsors who believe in the power of education.

We serve students across Ilocos Norte, Ilocos Sur, Cagayan, and Manila, helping them discover life-changing educational opportunities.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when registering, including your name, email, contact details, and educational background.

How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely for scholarship matching and platform improvement. We use your information to:

  • Match you with relevant scholarship opportunities
  • Facilitate communication between students and sponsors
  • Improve our platform services

Data Protection

We are committed to protecting your personal information. We do not share your information with third parties without your explicit consent. All data is stored securely and handled in accordance with data protection regulations.
